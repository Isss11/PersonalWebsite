Author, Waking the Red (March 2018 - Present)

I have written under both "Pamanci11" and most recently, "Isaiah Sinclair."

I currently write for Waking the Red in my free time. Waking the Red is Toronto FC's #1 fan website, and get press access at Toronto FC games.

Over the years, I have written numerous articles, including my "Who TFC Were" series, where I reminicse about previous players who played for Toronto FC and their contributions to the club.

Pamanci, YouTube Channel (December 2018 - August 2021)

On Pamanci, I've made varying YouTube videos on North America soccer. These primarily include historical videos, such as my series on South Floridan Soccer History. This series included 10 videos from the Miami Gatos of the 1970s to the creation of Inter Miami in 2018. I have also covered numerous videos on local Canadian soccer, from the now defunct K-W United of the PDL to Toronto FC of Major League Soccer.
