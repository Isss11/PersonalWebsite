To see our presentation, click here.

The Random Art Generator is a program that algorithmically develops a randomized piece of modern art. Generally speaking, it is created byd eveloping mini-squares within the square-shaped artwork, and fills them with different random shapes of different colours and transparencies. They are then connected together in a visual array placed on a single image to create the final artpiece.

Technologies Used: Python and Tkinter
